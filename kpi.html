KPI Framework & Monitoring Plan

Measurable indicators and tracking mechanisms for each digital dimension — enabling quarterly progress reviews.

Why KPIs Matter

Without measurable targets, improvement is invisible. These KPIs translate survey scores into operational metrics that ICT, Library, and Academic teams can track continuously.

Measurable

Each KPI has a clear numeric target — no ambiguity about what success looks like.

Time-bound

Monitoring plans specify frequency — monthly, quarterly, or per semester.

Owned

Each dimension maps to a responsible team — ICT, Library, or Academic Registry.

Actionable

KPI breaches trigger specific interventions from the recommendations roadmap.

Monitoring Calendar

When each KPI should be reviewed and by whom.

Monthly

WiFi Uptime Report

ICT Infrastructure team reviews network logs, uptime %, and speed test results. Escalate if uptime drops below 99%.

Monthly

Clearance Turnaround Report

Academic Registry pulls average approval times from admin system. Flag any cases exceeding 48 hours.

Quarterly

Emhare Usability Survey

Short pulse survey sent to all students. Track % rating Emhare as "easy to use". Compare to previous quarter.

Per Semester

Google Classroom Usage Analytics

Platform analytics report: active users, login frequency, assignment submission rates. Target ≥80% active users.

Annually

Full Digital Maturity Re-Assessment

Re-run the full survey across all groups. Re-cluster and re-classify. Compare composite maturity scores to baseline.
