Feature Importance

Random Forest reveals which dimensions drive the maturity classification.

1
WiFi Reliability

~0.90 importance — by far the dominant predictor. Whether a student is Emerging or Established is almost entirely determined by their WiFi experience.

2
Emhare Ease

~0.05 importance — contributes modestly. Portal usability matters but is not the differentiator.

3
Clearance Process

~0.03 importance — nearly identical scores across both clusters mean it adds little discriminatory power.

4
Google Classroom

~0.02 importance — confidence grows with maturity but is not a strong separator at this stage.

Cluster Validation — Silhouette Scores

k=2 gives the highest silhouette score, confirming the binary maturity split is the most natural grouping.
